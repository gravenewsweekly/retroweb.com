Welcome to RetroWeb

Welcome to RetroWeb — a tribute to the golden age of the internet. In an era when websites were simple, clean, and focused on content, browsing felt effortless. At RetroWeb, we bring back that nostalgia with a **minimalistic design** that prioritizes **ease of use, readability, and a clutter-free experience**.

Unlike modern websites overloaded with **ads, pop-ups, and distractions**, RetroWeb is built for **pure content consumption**. Whether you experienced the early days of the web or are just curious about its roots, this platform lets you **explore the history, culture, and evolution of the internet** in a distraction-free environment.

Step into a digital time machine and rediscover **how the internet shaped global communication, technology, and society**. Here, content is king—just as it was in the early 2000s.
