About Us

RetroWeb is a tribute to the early days of the internet, when websites were simple, clean, and focused on content. Our mission is to bring back the nostalgia of the 2000s era while providing a modern, user-friendly experience.

Founded in 2025, RetroWeb was created by a group of internet enthusiasts who believe in the power of simplicity. We aim to create a space that reminds people of the early days of the web, when things were less cluttered and more meaningful.
