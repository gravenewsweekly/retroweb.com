Ads Policy

RetroWeb is committed to providing a clean and user-friendly experience. This Ads Policy outlines the guidelines and principles we follow for displaying advertisements on our platform.

  • Relevance: Ads displayed on RetroWeb are relevant to our users' interests and preferences.
  • Transparency: All ads are clearly labeled as "Sponsored" or "Advertisement" to distinguish them from organic content.
  • Quality: We ensure that ads meet high-quality standards and do not contain misleading or offensive content.
  • Privacy: User data is never shared with advertisers without explicit consent. We adhere to strict privacy guidelines.
  • Frequency: Ads are displayed in moderation to avoid disrupting the user experience.
  • Third-Party Ads: We partner with trusted third-party ad networks to deliver relevant and safe advertisements.
  • User Control: Users can provide feedback on ads or opt out of personalized advertising through their account settings.
  • Compliance: All ads comply with applicable laws and regulations, including those related to data protection and consumer rights.
